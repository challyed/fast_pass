This application is in alpha.

The Las Vegas Development FastPass is currently in active development by 2013 Code for America fellows and is available for demo and testing purposes. Many features may be incomplete or broken. To contribute, please check out our GitHub repository or e-mail us. Thanks!

Find the best property for your business.

Am I zoned for my barbershop? Is this a great spot for my bar? Where should I open my boutique hat store? This tool will help you answer these important questions before starting the permitting and licensing process with the City of Las Vegas.

Important note

This tool does not automatically submit any forms or applications to the City. However, we'll connect you with appropriate forms or contacts at the end of this process.

What you'll need

  • A business idea
  • Details about your business activities
  • (optional) An address or neighborhood where you'd like to locate
